What’s the Deal?

Aotearoa is beautiful. Our forests and green spaces are humming with unique and beautiful life. They allow us to breathe, eat, explore, play and connect. They have given us so much. It is our turn to give back.

The consequences of long-overlooked climate destruction are starting to show. Our cities may be expanding but at what cost? Protecting and preserving New Zealand's flora and fauna has never been more crucial than now. We have the ability and responsibility to shape our future and to create a better world for current and future generations.

To show our love, this is a space to share our memories, hopes and loves—appreciating all that nature brings, from the deepest bush to our local gardens, to remember why we should take action to protect the place we call home.

Don’t Just Talk the Talk

Sometimes we feel like we can’t enact change, but every action plants a seed for a better future. We’ve listed some simple things you can do to give back. Why not give one a go?

Volunteer

Get stuck into hands-on volunteering opportunities such as tree planting days and clean-ups. These activities foster community and are a great activity to do with friends.

Donate

Not wanting to get your hands dirty? Why not donate and support local causes so they can continue all their hard work in protecting and preserving our natural landscapes.

Sustainable Swaps

Consider how you can reduce your impact on nature in your daily life. Walk or take the bus, use a reusable cup for your morning coffee and opt for more sustainable products.

Plant a Garden

Time to get outside and create a garden at home. Include native plants and flowers to bring endemic species and pollinators to your little forest. It may be hard work, but it will be rewarding and you will have a little oasis for yourself to enjoy.
